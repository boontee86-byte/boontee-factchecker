Overall Verdict
MISLEADING

Summary

This video presents a grand conspiracy theory of global financial history, claiming that economic collapses are deliberately engineered by a shadowy network of 'transnational capital.' The host explicitly frames the content as speculation with 'no scholarly basis.' While many historical anchors are broadly accurate (Bank of England 1694, Glass-Steagall repeal 1999, BIS-Nazi connections, Paulson's 2008 profits, China's banking rise), these facts are selectively assembled and distorted to support an unfalsifiable conspiracy framework. Key errors include misdating the Federal Reserve's creation (says 1914, actually 1913), misnaming Jamie Dimon as 'Jimmy Diamond,' and presenting unverifiable claims about the BIS secretly orchestrating China's rise and now engineering a US economic collapse to benefit Israel. The content is best understood as ideologically-motivated historical revisionism rather than analysis.

Claims Analyzed (15)

"Andrew Ross Sorkin wrote a book called "1929" about the stock market collapse"
TRUE
Sorkin's book "1929: Inside the Greatest Crash in Wall Street History and How It Shattered a Nation" was published in 2025 and became a #1 New York Times bestseller.
https://www.amazon.com/1929-Inside-Greatest-History-Shattered/dp/0593296966https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1929_(book)
One of the few factually accurate specific attributions in the video.
"Financial collapses are deliberately engineered by transnational capital elites and do not happen naturally"
UNVERIFIABLE
The host himself labels this "speculation" with "no scholarly basis." While specific actors like John Paulson profit from collapses, systematic secret coordination lacks credible evidence. The Financial Crisis Inquiry Commission attributed the 2008 crisis to systemic failures, deregulation, and regulatory capture — not deliberate engineering.
https://fcic-static.law.stanford.edu/cdn_media/fcic-reports/fcic_final_report_full.pdf
This is the central unfalsifiable thesis of the video. The host's own disclaimer makes this self-evidently speculative.
"Banks can "print their own money" — a bank with $1M in deposits ends up with $2M after lending it out"
MISLEADING
Banks do create money through credit creation (a real phenomenon). But the claim a bank "has $2M" after lending misrepresents balance sheets — it has $1M in liabilities offset by $1M in loan assets. The Bank of England has published clear explanations of money creation.
https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/quarterly-bulletin/2014/q1/money-creation-in-the-modern-economy
The underlying concept (credit creation) is real; the specific numerical claim is misleading.
"The 1688 Glorious Revolution brought Dutch financial wealth to England, leading to the Bank of England in 1694"
MOSTLY TRUE
Historians confirm that William of Orange's ascension enabled transplantation of Dutch financial innovations into England, including a central bank, funded national debt, and stock market. The Bank of England was founded in 1694 to fund the war against France.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glorious_Revolutionhttps://www.bankofengland.co.uk/about/history
Historically accurate in outline. The conspiratorial framing of this as an elite wealth transfer is the host's interpretation.
"The Bank of England was founded in 1694 as a private bank not accountable to the public"
TRUE
The Bank of England was incorporated in 1694 as a private joint-stock bank and remained privately owned until nationalised by the Attlee government in 1946. It was founded to lend money to the government to fund the war against France.
https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/about/historyhttps://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bank_of_England
"John Rockefeller, Carnegie, JP Morgan, and Vanderbilt were "agents" of the City of London sent to monopolise America"
FALSE
These were American industrialists who built their own empires. While JP Morgan had British connections (his father Junius Spencer Morgan was London-based), calling them deliberate agents of the City of London sent to colonise America is a conspiracy framing without historical support.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/J._P._Morganhttps://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_D._Rockefeller
JP Morgan did have trans-Atlantic banking connections, but this is vastly different from being a deliberate agent of the Bank of England.
"The Federal Reserve was created in 1914"
MOSTLY FALSE
The Federal Reserve Act was signed into law by President Woodrow Wilson on December 23, 1913. The twelve Federal Reserve banks opened operations in November 1914, which may be the source of confusion, but the institution was legally created in 1913.
https://www.federalreservehistory.org/essays/federal-reserve-act-signedhttps://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Reserve_Act
A factual error. The Fed was created in 1913, not 1914.
"Bill Clinton repealed the Glass-Steagall Act in 1999"
MOSTLY TRUE
Clinton signed the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act on November 12, 1999, which repealed key Glass-Steagall provisions removing barriers between commercial banking, investment banking, and insurance. The bill passed the Senate 90–8 and House 362–57, making it broadly bipartisan.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gramm%E2%80%93Leach%E2%80%93Bliley_Acthttps://www.federalreservehistory.org/essays/gramm-leach-bliley-act
Economists debate whether the repeal was a primary cause of 2008. The Cato Institute has argued it was not; others disagree.
"John Paulson made $20 billion by betting against the housing market in 2008"
MISLEADING
Paulson's firm made ~$15 billion in 2007 by shorting subprime mortgage securities; Paulson personally earned ~$4 billion that year. Some sources cite $20 billion as aggregate across 2007–2008, conflating firm vs. personal profits. The claim he caused the collapse is unsupported — he bet on an existing systemic failure.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Paulsonhttps://stockburry.com/the-king-of-big-short-john-paulson-made-26-billion-in-2008-crisis/
The video uses this to claim the collapse was engineered, but Paulson's short position was a bet against the market, not a mechanism to trigger collapse.
"JPMorgan Chase (called "Jimmy Diamond" in the video) became America's largest bank by buying collapsed banks"
MISLEADING
Jamie Dimon (not "Jimmy Diamond") led JPMorgan's acquisitions of Bear Stearns and Washington Mutual during the crisis. JPMorgan is now the largest US bank. However, the Bear Stearns deal was facilitated by the Federal Reserve and Treasury to prevent systemic collapse — it was not purely opportunistic predation.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jamie_Dimonhttps://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/JPMorgan_Chase
The host mispronounces/misremembers the CEO's name as "Jimmy Diamond" throughout. Jamie Dimon is the correct name.
"The Bank for International Settlements (BIS) is in Basel, Switzerland and acted as a financial channel for Nazi Germany during WWII"
MOSTLY TRUE
The BIS is indeed based in Basel. Historical records confirm it transferred Czech gold to the Nazi Reichsbank in 1939, facilitated transactions between Nazi Germany and neutral countries, and accepted looted gold. A Bretton Woods resolution called for abolition of the BIS, but it survived and grew more powerful.

FALSE
The yuan did appreciate post-2008 and China grew rapidly, but this resulted from China's trade surpluses, US diplomatic pressure to revalue the yuan, and Chinese government stimulus — not secret BIS coordination. The BIS does not set or coordinate member nations' exchange rates.

The BIS coordinates regulatory standards (like Basel Accords) but does not direct national exchange rate policy.

Game Theory #17: The Great Reset

Channel: Predictive History | Published: March 31, 2026

---

Introduction

This video, episode 17 in the "Game Theory" series by the YouTube channel Predictive History, presents a sweeping, conspiratorial interpretation of global financial history — from the 1688 Glorious Revolution through to the 2008 financial crisis and into the present day. The host explicitly frames the content as speculation and entertainment with "no scholarly basis," yet goes on to make numerous specific factual and historical claims. The central thesis is that economic booms and busts are deliberately engineered by a shadowy network of "transnational capital" — comprising institutions like the Bank for International Settlements (BIS), the City of London, and Wall Street — and that the next engineered collapse will transfer global financial dominance from the United States to Israel.

---

The Boom-Bust Cycle and the Conspiracy Thesis

The host begins by explaining the standard economic concept of the boom-bust cycle — the idea that capitalism naturally oscillates between periods of growth and contraction — before immediately dismissing it. Citing Andrew Ross Sorkin's book 1929, he argues that the standard explanation (collective delusion and overconfidence cause bubbles to pop) is insufficient. The "real" answer, he proposes, is that financial collapses are engineered by powerful actors who control the levers of the global economy.

---

How Banks Create Money

The host explains fractional reserve banking with the example of a bank that takes in $1 million in deposits, lends it out, and ends up with $2 million. While simplified, this captures the basic mechanism of credit creation. He then argues that interest rates function not as signals to consumers but as coordination mechanisms telling banks whether to expand or contract the money supply.

---

The Glorious Revolution and the Birth of the Bank of England

The host traces the origins of the current financial system to the 1688 Glorious Revolution, in which William of Orange brought Dutch financial innovations to England — leading to the founding of the Bank of England in 1694. He identifies three key features of this system: profits are privatised and losses socialised; wealth is generated through activity (particularly wars); and capital must flow freely across borders. He credits philosophers John Locke (empiricism, property rights), David Hume (skepticism), and Jeremy Bentham / John Stuart Mill (utilitarianism) with providing ideological cover for materialism, and adds that Marx, Darwin, and Freud further stripped the divine from public consciousness.

---

Transnational Capital Comes to America

The host argues that after conquering Britain, transnational capital moved to America through agents — Rockefeller, Carnegie, JP Morgan, and Vanderbilt — who used City of London capital to monopolise American industry. These figures then created the Federal Reserve System (dated to 1914 in the video, though the actual date is 1913). After the Fed's creation, America entered WWI, experienced the 1929 crash, and then entered WWII — events the host implies were engineered by the same financial elite.

---

The Bank for International Settlements and Nazi Germany

The host introduces the BIS, headquartered in Basel, Switzerland, as the unaccountable "central bank of central banks." Drawing from Tower of Basel, he argues that Nazi Germany relied on the BIS as a financial channel to neutral countries (Sweden, Switzerland) to purchase raw materials — and that this explains why Germany did not invade Switzerland. Despite a Bretton Woods resolution calling for the BIS's abolition, the bank survived and became more powerful after the war.

---

The 2008 Financial Crisis: Engineered Collapse

The host attributes the 2008 crisis to two enabling factors: Bill Clinton's push for minority homeownership and the 1999 repeal of the Glass-Steagall Act (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act). Combined with massive foreign capital inflows — from China, the GCC, Japan's yen carry trade, and European pension funds — this created demand for CDOs backed by subprime mortgages.

His central claim: the collapse was deliberately triggered. His evidence — John Paulson made $20 billion by betting against the housing market using credit default swaps, and JPMorgan (led by "Jimmy Diamond," a mispronunciation of Jamie Dimon) consolidated the banking industry by acquiring failing institutions on the cheap. After 2008, institutional investors dramatically increased their share of US home ownership as individual homeowners lost their properties.

---

China's Rise as an Engineered Transition

The host argues that the BIS orchestrated China's economic rise after 2008 through coordination of yuan appreciation — a signalling mechanism encouraging global trade with China. China's bank lending exploded post-2008, China became the dominant global manufacturing exporter by 2024, and China's four largest banks (ICBC, CCB, ABC, BOC) are now the largest in the world by assets. The transition stalled, however, because China does not want the responsibility of global reserve currency status or overseas military basing.

---

The Next Engineered Collapse: America to Israel

In the video's most speculative section, the host argues that transnational capital will next engineer a collapse of the US economy — using the private credit bubble (~$2 trillion) or the AI bubble — and shift capital to Israel. His reasoning: Israel is engaged in the "Greater Israel Project" (territorial expansion), will need capital to rebuild after wars, and will become a global trade hub. He frames Donald Trump's MAGA movement as America's attempt to expel the "parasite" of transnational capital — a painful but ultimately beneficial process for the American host nation.

---

Conclusion

The video weaves together genuine historical facts, legitimate economic concepts, and evidence-free conspiracy claims. Its historical anchors — the 1688 Glorious Revolution, the 1694 Bank of England, the 1929 crash, the Federal Reserve's creation, the BIS's wartime conduct, and the 2008 crisis — are largely accurate in outline though frequently distorted in detail. The conspiracy framework layered on top — that transnational capital deliberately engineers wars and collapses and is now planning a shift from America to Israel — is speculative, unsubstantiated, and in places demonstrably false. The host himself acknowledges this is speculation with no scholarly basis.
